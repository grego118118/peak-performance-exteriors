Good to Know

Common Questions

How much does a new roof cost?

It depends on the size, pitch, and condition of what's underneath. That's why estimates are free — we come out, look at the actual roof, and give you a real number, not a guess over the phone.

Do you handle emergency roof repairs?

Yes — we offer 24-hour emergency call service. If a storm opens up your roof at 2 AM, call. We'll get it protected first and talk about the permanent fix in daylight.

What warranty do you offer?

Every roof comes with a full 10-year labor warranty on top of the manufacturer's material warranty. If something we installed fails, we fix it — that's the whole warranty.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — Massachusetts Home Improvement Contractor #190959 and Construction Supervisor License CS-105982, fully insured, with OSHA-trained crews on every job.

Do you do more than roofs?

Yes — siding, gutters and flashing, skylights, and exterior home improvements. If it's on the outside of your house, it's worth a call.

Do you work in the winter?

Year-round. We also do roof snow removal — heavy snow loads and ice dams don't wait for spring, and neither do we.
